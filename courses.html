Course Objectives

“90% MBA graduates in India are unemployable.“

After the completion of the training, you will be able to

1. Understand the HR management function in details

2. Understand the Jobs & Responsibilities of HR department

3. Work independently for any HR management function in real life situation

4. Find out a suitable HR jobs of your choice

5. Launch your dream HR career

6. Brush up your skills of HR and take your knowledge to the next level


Who should go for this Course?

1. Fresh Graduate from any stream, who wants to launch their HR career

2. Fresh MBA (HR), who is yet to get a HR job due to lack of practical, real life HR skills

3. Fresh MBA (Any), who wants to shift into HR domain

4. Experienced Professionals from any background, any domain or any industry

5. HR Recruiters who wants to start career in Generalist HR

6. HR Professionals, who want to sharpen & upgrade their HR skills to get faster growth.


Eligibility Check & Admission Process

Interested candidates have to undergo the below admission process to undertake the training.

1. Online test on basic English, Mathematics & Logical reasoning

2. Personal interview

3. Collection of Admission forms

4. Filling up of admission form & Document submission


What are the pre-requisites for this Course?

This course does not require any specific pre-requisites, any HR knowledge or any experience or a functional skill set. Course Content


The below modules will be covered in the training program.

1. Recruitment & Sourcing

2. Compensation & Benefits

3. Payroll Management

4. Performance Management

5. HR Operation

6. Labour Laws

7. Statutory Compliances

8. One to One Career Session

9. Mock Interview

10. MS-Excel
